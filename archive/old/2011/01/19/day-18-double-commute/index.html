Day 18 - Double commute

19 Jan 2011

I guess the grim hurt more than I realised, I had a disturbed nights sleep as both kids decided to wake me up at some point during the night.  I then couldn't go back to sleep and my legs were so achey I had to take some Ibuprofen!  I'm over doing it, so I decided the 18th would be a rest day, which in reality meant Boris Biking it to and from work.

I still love the Boris bikes and I've traded 30 mins of daily tube crush for under 30 mins of exhilarating pedalling.   I'm quite chilled by nature but I'm wary of cars and vans, so I accept they will not notice me, they will cut me up and / or try and run me over; so I cycle defensively.  However, occasionally find myself wanting to overtake all cyclists in front of me - is that normal?
