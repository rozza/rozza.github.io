Good run with Pete we volunteered to come out and support me whilst doing Janathon and as a keen cyclist he was slightly anxious about running and pace; well no need he set a good pace and I had to slow him down as I was suffering!

My knee is feeling a bit better after cooling down and stretches - so hopefully it'll cope with the rest of janathon.

Note to self: don't eat tea an hour before going for a run; running with a food baby makes it much harder work!
