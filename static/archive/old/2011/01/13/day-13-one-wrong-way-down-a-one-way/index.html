Day 13 - One wrong way down a one way

13 Jan 2011

Boris Bikes rule!  I got to Kings X but alas my first experience at 7:30am was an empty dock, so a quick jaunter over to the nearest dock and vola! I was in charge of a bike.   The bike is sturdy to say the least, but I reckon it was better than the last bike I owned, so 12 minutes and one wrong way down a one way later, I had reached my work - simples!  Next time I'll have a better route.

The only downside today was on leaving the office tonight it was chucking it down, so I opted for the crush of the tube and I feel guilty about it - go figure?!
