Day 22 - Gym will fix it

22 Jan 2011

Well today I went to the gym for 45 minutes.  The plan for the gym is to focus on upper body and core strength, I figure the running is good for the legs and cycling will be to!  So after beasting myself on the weights (doing relatively small weights on the various torture machines) my arms, chest and back are now tender (read f'ing hurt).  So much so I'm really glad I have power steering, as I could really feel it straight after on the drive home!  I'm hoping this is a newbie thing and that I will soon get used to doing weights and be able to lift weights that someone my size should be able to!
